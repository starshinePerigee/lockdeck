When you beat a level, you unlock a chest. Treasure inside helps you refine your hand:

## Artifacts: single use consumables

Artifacts are powerful single use consumables.

The are shown at the top of the screen, with a hovertext guide.

ex:
- toothpick: break instead of a pick

When you click one, the guide is shown, as well as "keep" and "sell: 1🪙"

If you have more than three (?) artifacts, or already have that specific artifact, keep is greyed out


## Pick modifications

When you pick up a gem/jewelry, a number of picks are chosen at random to apply the effect to.
You can also pick a coin to sell the gem/jewelry.

(Will need to have a highlight preview in the UI)

### Jewelry: apply pick effects

See pick effect brainstorm

Pick effects are shown on card as a custom handle, as well as an prefix. ("strong", "sneaky")

Picks can only have one effect - prioritize effectless picks when picking up jewelry, but if not enough allow overwriting effects.


### Gems: upgrade the pick

Each pick effect has a corresponding gem. (Force is topaz, etc)

Effects have a hidden "priority" level, which will set where on the pick they apply.
Force and jump are the same, but are both higher than reveal so you don't reveal then immediately force past it. Jam always goes on the bottom.

Gems have a preset pattern they add or remove. For instance, a topaz might look like "+1 +2 -1 force"

Trend toward removals - built-in picks should be overbuilt and splashy 

Each gem adds a "+1" to the pick's name.


## Coins: buy new picks

After you loot treasure, there are two icons: deck and shop

Deck: view your deck. Lets you trash picks for free.

Shop: see standard picks. Lets you buy picks for coins.
Has one (?) random pick plus the standard spread. (Buying it shows another?)

### Flow and balance

Coins are your reserve health - they're how you "heal", buying picks back up to a healthy number.
