If a pick breaks in game, does it break IN REAL LIFE???

(ie: if a pick breaks in a round, is it permanently removed from your deck)
- this was the original intent
- it's theoretically possible to no-break a round with careful picking

This does lead to potential issues where you intentionally break a pick to remove it
- could be mitigated with meta design:
-- free trashing
-- picks are always good

Decision: yes
- between rounds, you can visit your deck and throw out any picks you don't like
