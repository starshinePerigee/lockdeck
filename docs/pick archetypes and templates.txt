What pick archetypes are useful?
- bulk push - move lots of pins in safety / emergency
- light push to close out a position
- tall push to skip danger
- mid push filler pick
- broad hints to give you basic outline
- tall hints to line up safe skips after reveal
- narrow hints to identify threats conclusively
- small jams for tricks
- large jams for protection of cleared pins
- reveal and crush to deal with upcoming threats
-- reveal + hint + jump = safe
-- crush is useful as a defense against particularly difficult layouts
