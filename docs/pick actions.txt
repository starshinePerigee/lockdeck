force x: push pin up x  (wedges)
bump x: create a hint for x  (circles)
jam: resist x motion (including sliding)  (hooks)

(depths can include these also!)


Sliding happens two turns after a pin is touched.
turn 0: you force 2 the first pin. it is now position 2
turn 1: cooldown
turn 2: shaking
turn 3: move 1
