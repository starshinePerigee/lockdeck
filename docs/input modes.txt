Drag:
- on hover: card is highlighted
- on drag: while dragging, effect is previewed
- on release: card is applied (if in keyway) dropped (elsewhere) trashed (trash)

Taps:
- on tap: card is highlighted
- tap keyway: preview, tap again to apply

Tap and hover:
- on tap: card is highlighted
- on hover keyway: preview
- on tap keyway: apply
